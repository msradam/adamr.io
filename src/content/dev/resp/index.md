App for first responders to deliver psychological first aid in the immediate aftermath of a disaster, following the WHO's Psychological First Aid guidelines. Three modules: Survey (structured intake questionnaires that build rapport and populate an anonymized database), Status (a catalog tracking each survivor's check-in, geolocation, and mental-health risk indicators, with family-reunification matching), and Services. Winner of IBM Call for Code @ AngelHack 2019.

An epidemiological simulation of mass incarceration with a synthetic population network. Developed from initial research called [Jails by Java](https://www.semanticscholar.org/paper/Jails-by-Java%3A-Simulating-Racial-Disparities-in-and-Rahman-Schweitzer/0e859f3304d80e26ef75b9944250d46511b078bf), winner of the MIT INSPIRE Political Science award (Dec. 2015) and Social Good Award + 2nd Place Crowd Favorite at Wellesley Hacks 2017.

A multi-agent civic-AI system that produces flood-exposure briefings for NYC neighborhoods with every factual claim cited back to a public-record source. Combines IBM Granite 4.1 for reasoning, NASA/IBM Prithvi-EO and TerraMind geospatial foundation models for satellite-derived exposure features, and a Mellea-coordinated agent graph that retrieves from FEMA flood maps, NYC DEP infrastructure data, and historical event records. Built for AMD MI300X; designed so a community board member or local journalist can trace any line in the briefing to the document it came from.
